Hobbies Page

I’ve known Anthonie since I was a freshman in college. He wanted to pursue acting for fun and possibly a career. I wanted to be a screenwriter and director as a career. We both have love for arts, music, and basketball so we spent time together talking about all those things. We had similar hobbies, when I went to his dorm and saw his artbook, immediately I knew he was a talented person because he can draw really well. It was my sophomore year and his junior year when he started making hats. I’ve watched him create his first fashion show and have a great turnout of what seemed to be like 200 people at least that came to see the show. It was very impressive for it being his first show. His dedication had lead to others supporting and helping him along the way. He started making a name for himself in Albuquerque and not just on campus at the University of New Mexico. By the time it was junior year many people started buying his hats all over campus and he would make custom made hats for my friends. People would share it all over social media and spread the word on how to get custom made hats by Anthonie. It was cool because they actually are high quality hats for the same price of the ones we normally get at the big corporate stores. As we went through college together I saw that his hobby of making art has turned into a business.
